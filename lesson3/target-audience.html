Target audience

The target audience for this website is people from teenagers to seniors who go outside. Utah has a lot of National parks, trails, and other outdoor places that attract people of a younger age to visit. It is important to know what to expect from the weather during your visit. Parents want to make sure their kids will be warm enough or have sunscreen during their outside activities. Elderly people care about themselves and other family members and want to have and make warm clothes for everybody.
