Site purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide Utah's residents and visitors with information about the weather in that beautiful state. Utah has a lot of National parks and trails and it is important for each person to be prepared for the weather conditions. The families with the kids want to have fun outside and each member needs to have the right clothes and sun/ wind protection.
